So you have found the secret sauce....



The idea behind the hot sauce is that your food is already seasoned -- you don't have a need to add any extra seasoning if you want to add a little kick. The result is my habanero hot sauce (which isn't such a huge secret).

Use:
• Be very cautious when opening!!!! Use a paper towel and when opening for the first time and open over the sink!
• Refrigerate
• Shake well before use
• (Please) Use with caution !
