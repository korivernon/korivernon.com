CS-1114 - Introduction to Programming Website

Computer Science is not the easiest thing to get the hang of. At times it can be difficult and you may feel like you hit a dead end; however, you deserve to be here (at NYU)! CS-1114 is designed to give students an Introduction into Computer Science. As a Teaching Assistant for CS-1114, it is my goal to make sure you are challenged, prepapred, and feel comfortable with the course material. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out to me through email!

Tips for CS-1114

Class

Go to it and participate lol. it's not that hard. Also, if you have any questions it's likely in the syllabus (I would link the syllabus but it's not my property to share!).

Lab

Labs are meant to be hard but they're also meant to be finished in one lab period. Labs may be one of the best ways to prepare for tests, as the questions are free response; always try to finish your labs outside of lab if you are unable to finish them in class.
