Tips for CS-1114

Class

Go to it and participate lol. it's not that hard. Also, if you have any questions it's likely in the syllabus (I would link the syllabus but it's not my property to share!).

Lab

Labs are meant to be hard but they're also meant to be finished in one lab period. Labs may be one of the best ways to prepare for tests, as the questions are free response; always try to finish your labs outside of lab if you are unable to finish them in in class.
