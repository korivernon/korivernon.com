NSBE S.P.E.C.

Hey NSBE Fam! I'm your 2020 Special Projects and Entrepreneurship Chair (S.P.E.C.) The 2020 school year is coming to an end and we have some announcements to share! Please feel free to text me or email me! I'm always available for you all!

First of all, if you haven’t already, fill out the interest form for the NSBE Hackathon Team and BLAC Projects! If you’d like to learn more about the Hackathon Team or BLAC Projects, check out the links below!

Start Prep Early!


Event 1 - Academic Technical Bowl

The Academic Technical Bowl basically tests students’ in their respective fields. Maximum Cash Prize of $1000! To see a basic overview of what they offer, check out their Google Doc!


Event 2 - NSBE Debaters

NSBE Debaters - Debate team consisting of a team of three people!


Event 3 - NSBE Fire Pit

NSBE entrepreneurs are invited to form teams and compete for awards, prizes, and the opportunity to pitch their idea to seasoned entrepreneurs, investors, and potential business partners. Maximum Cash Prize of $500. This will be intertwined with BLAC Projects!


Event 4 - Technical Research Exhibition

Technical Research Exhibition - The Technical Research Exhibition (TRE) is designed to showcase the NSBE members' technical accomplishments and technical communication skills. This program both recognizes accomplishment and provides a forum for growth. Two tracks allow for recognition of student accomplishment at various stages of a project. Maximum Cash Prize of $1000.
