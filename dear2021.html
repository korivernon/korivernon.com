Dear Wake Young Men's Leadership Academy Class of 2021

Congratulations for graduating!

Life is an awesome time. College is an awesome time. Regardless of what you choose as your path in life, I urge you to take advantage of every thing you come across. Build lifelong connections. Have fun. Make mistakes. Learn from them. Don’t be afraid to put yourself out there. Remember where you come from and lean on your brothers in hard times. Even when times get hard, remember, You got this.

You all truly inspired me with your quotes. I can’t wait see the amazing things that you all do in the future.

Please feel free to reach out and contact me if you have any questions or just want someone to talk to.

My line is always open for my brothers!
